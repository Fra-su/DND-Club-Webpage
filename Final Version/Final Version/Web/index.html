Rangitoto D&D Club


D&D at Rangitoto College

At Rangitoto College we have a variety of clubs, and D&D is one of them. Dungeons and Dragons helps students develop problem-solving skills, confidence, creativity, and much much more. The club is open to all year levels and all levels of experience—whether you're a complete beginner or an experienced adventurer. Join us to roll the dice, tell epic stories, and be part of a community where imagination leads the way.


D&D Times

D&D is open in M9 and M10 on Mondays, Wednesdays, and Fridays from 3:30PM to 5:00PM.

If you are interested, you can contact our leaders or join our Discord server!
